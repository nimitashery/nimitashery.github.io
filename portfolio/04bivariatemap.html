    Opportunity Cities

    About: This map looks at the new trend of migration of residents from high-cost coastal cities to more affordable inland cities, especially since the COVID-19 pandemic began. Three of the most trending new cities, Austin, Raleigh and Denver are compared with each other using the Change in Population (2010-2019) and Cost of Living Index data.

    Tools: ArcGIS Pro, Illustrator
