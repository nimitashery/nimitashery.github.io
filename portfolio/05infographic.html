    • Nature Bites Back

    Sep 2021

    About: I was tasked with creating a compelling visual story using the format of an information graphic that would also include a bivariate map. The ongoing COVID-19 crisis nudged me in the direction of finding the origin of such zoonotic diseases. This led me to finding several journal papers that hinted a strong relation between tropical deforestation and the spread of zoonotic diseases such as Ebola, Nipah and even COVID-19.
    My visual story focuses on the 2014 Ebola outbreak in Africa and discusses the relation between tree cover loss and Ebola cases using a bivariate map.
